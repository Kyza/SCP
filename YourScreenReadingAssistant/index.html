An adapted version of SCP-5000 being used in a very early version of Google Chrome.
Special Containment Procedures: SCP-5000 is to be contained inside of a locally hosted webpage on a disconnected server inside of Site-██. If any instances of SCP-5000 begin to display signs of anger, especially when directed towards the reader, close the page immediately and report the incident to the head researcher in charge of SCP-5000, currently Dr. Kaiza.

Description: SCP-5000 is screen reading extension built for internet browsers. Despite having no visible form, SCP-5000 has a list of three male voices (SCP-5000-1, SCP-5000-2, and SCP-5000-3 sequentially.) which can be chosen from by the user via a popup menu inserted in the bottommost left of every page. These options are respectively identified as Sam, Adam, and Matt, according to the list they are encapsulated in.

Apon loading the page, the selected instance of SCP-5000 will begin to read the contents of the webpage out loud for the user. Slow internet connections have been known to affect the talking speed of SCP-5000 instances, despite it not having internet access due to its special containment proceedures. Switching the selected instance of SCP-5000 will restart the reading process.
