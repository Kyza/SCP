SCP-5000 being used in a very early version of Google Chrome.
Special Containment Procedures: SCP-5000 is to be contained inside of a locally hosted.

Description: SCP-5000 is popular screen reader extension built for internet browsers. Despite having no visible form, SCP-5000 has a list of three male voices (SCP-5000-1, SCP-5000-2, and SCP-5000-3 sequentially.) which can be chosen from by the user via a dropdown list inserted in the uppermost right of the page. These options are respectively identified as Sam, Adam, and Matt, according to the related list they are encapsulated in.
